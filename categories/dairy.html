Lactose-Free

With more low-lactose and lactose-free options than ever, nothing's standing between you and dairy's nutritious, delicious benefits.

Milk

Milk offers nutrients and benefits to help you achieve your wellness goals. Get a glassful of information on milk and all its benefits. Milk contains nine essential nutrients like high-quality protein, calcium, vitamin D and more...

Cheese

With hundreds of varieties of cheese available, there is an option for almost every palate and diet. Cheese contains calcium and phosphorus, which can help build and maintain strong bones and teeth. It also contains protein, which helps build and repair muscles.

Yogurt

Go beyond breakfast and discover all yogurt has to offer throughout your day. Most yogurts are made with live and active cultures, and some also provide probiotics that help maintain digestive health.

Whey Protein

Whey is one of two major high-quality proteins naturally found in cow's milk. Whey contains all of the essential amino acids or   building blocks   your body needs.

Cream

Whether you add it to your morning pick me up or serve it on Taco Tuesday, chances are there's more than one type of cream for your tastes.

Butter

From salted and unsalted to whipped and clarified, butter can bring that extra burst of flavor to our favorite foods.

Ice Cream

Whether celebrating a special occasion with friends and family or making an ordinary moment special, ice cream is often the go-to treat for many.

Health Benefits of Dairy

CALCIUM

Dairy Value = 25%

Helps build and maintain strong bones and teeth.

RIBOFLAVIN

Helps maintain a healthy immune system, helps regulate metabolism and helps protect healthy cells from damage.

PROTEIN

Dairy Value = 16%

Helps build and repair tissue. Helps maintain a healthy immune system.

VITAMIN B12

Dairy Value = 50%

Helps with normal blood function, helps keep the nervous system healthy.

IODINE

Dairy Value = 60%

Necessary for proper bone and brain development during pregnancy and infancy, linked to cognitive function in childhood.

NIACIN
